1. How to use our M-PESA till number

    • Dial *234#
    • Enter 2 for Tariff Query
    • Choose option 5 for Lipa na M-PESA
    • Enter Paybill Number 8765325 or Merchant Till Number
    • Enter the amount charged by the business
    • You will receive an SMS notification of with the charges of
      what it will cost to make the payment for that amount for that Paybill number
